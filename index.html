Welcome to JasDevelops We focus on helping Black and Brown women get unstuck, get out of debt, so they can become their own CFO!

Our Vision

A world where Black and Brown women would stop having a broke mindset and instead create their own personal capital. Be their own banks, live a debt free lifestyle, and secure that financial freedom they may crave.

Our Mission

To make this vision happen. We education Black and Brown women who may not had received financial literacy while growing up, and as a result may not know how to budget, or know the definition of a FICO score (their net worth), or how to invest.

Meet the Founder

Jasmine Reese

CEO | Founder

Growing up and seeing how my parents handled their finances pushed me into having a poverty mindset. I believed that I will never have enough money to achieve my goals or build wealth. But then again, I did not know the definition of what wealth was, since I always heard from my family and friends that wealth was only for rich, white people or eventually we will win the state lottery so thereâ€™s no need to save or invest. When I joined the United States Army I had the mentality that I worked hard so therefore, I deserved to purchase anything I want. Little did I know that having that mentality racked me up $110,000 worth of debt and spoiler alert that $110,000 was not student loans. It was because I had a broke mindset, I didn't know anything about saving, because I knew I could depend on Social Security.

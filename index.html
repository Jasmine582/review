Welcome to JasDevelops We are a Personal Financial Consulting company that focus on become their own Chief financial officer in their own household. CFOs holds the top financial position in an organization and are responsible for tracking cash flow and financial planning and analyzing financial strengths and weaknesses.

Our Vision

A world where women would stop having a broke mindset and instead create their own personal capital. Be their own banks, live a debt free lifestyle, and secure that financial freedom they may crave.


Our Mission

To make Financial Literacy simple for women by educating them on three important topics:

  • Budgeting
  • Net Worth
  • Investing
